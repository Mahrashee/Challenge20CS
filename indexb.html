Hydration Tracking App

Blu

A Solution to Your Water Habits

The Problem

Current hydration tracking apps aren't engaging enough and fail to inspire users to be motivated to enter their hydration consumption.

Background Research

65% of people consume less than 5 cups of water per day.

Mild dehydration can lead to a reduction in cognitive performance by up to 30%.

The Solution

Gamifying the app will enhance user engagement and motivation, leading to an improvement in their water consumption goals.

Tools Used

Figma, Miro, Microsoft Suite

Primary Research

Research Objective

How can we design and evaluate a personalized coaching app with gamification features to improve water consumption and hydration habits among individuals?

Survey Results

None of the survey respondents are currently drinking the recommended 8 glasses of water per day.

Forgetfulness is the main challenge people face when trying to track water intake, followed by lack of motivation and being too busy.

64% of respondents don’t use any technology to track hydration.
